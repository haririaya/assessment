Welcome

This App will work on approaching the Collatz Sequence problem. The sequence can be defined as follows: Given a starting number, a chain will be created by applying the following formula on the numbers iteratively. If n is even, n will be divided by 2. Otherwise, it will be multiplied by 3 and then incremented by 1.

Please head to the menu button next to the header and pick one of the options.

Brute Force and Memoization are two approaches to finding the number under 1 million that will produce the longest chain. Each page explains the approach followed in solving the problem and outputs the results obtained by the approach.

Longest Chain Visualization option will show you the longest chain that starts with a number under 1 million.

Generate Chain will generate and visualize a chain created by the number you input. Homepage will redirect you to this screen. Have Fun!
